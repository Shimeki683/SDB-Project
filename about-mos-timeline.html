1972

1987

1991

1992

MOS FOODS SINGAPORE PTE LTD is established in Singapore.

1993

1995

MOS Burger opened at Bugis Junction.

1996

MOS Food Services shares are listed on the First Section of Tokyo Stock Exchange.

1998

1999
